Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

WhichPuppy ("we," "our," "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, Google, or email, we receive your email address and display name. We do not receive or store passwords for social sign-in providers.

Location Data: We collect your country, region, and city (coarse location only) to provide region-specific breed cost estimates, availability, and climate-based recommendations. We do not track your precise GPS coordinates continuously. Location is captured once during setup and only updated when you manually change it.

Questionnaire Responses: Your answers about lifestyle, family, budget, and preferences are stored securely to generate personalized breed recommendations.

Subscription Data: We store your subscription status (free or premium) to manage access to premium features. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not see or store your payment information.

Analytics: We collect anonymous usage events such as questionnaire completion, breed views, and feature usage to improve the app. These events are not used for advertising.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide breed recommendations, cost estimates, and app functionality. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or tracking across other apps or websites.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud database) with row-level security policies ensuring you can only access your own data. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: AI-powered breed explanations (premium feature only). Anonymized questionnaire data may be sent to OpenAI to generate insights. Privacy Policy
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple, subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Google: Sign in with Google. Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

WhichPuppy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

AI Disclaimer

Last updated: April 2026

WhichPuppy uses artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance certain features of the app. This disclosure explains what AI does, what it does not do, and how you should interpret AI-generated content.

How We Use AI

WhichPuppy uses AI (specifically OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model) to generate certain premium content, including:

  • Personalized explanations of breed compatibility dimensions
  • Reality-check insights about owning specific breeds
  • Daily routine previews for puppy and adult life stages
  • Common mistakes new owners make with specific breeds

What AI Does NOT Do

The core breed recommendations, compatibility scores, and cost estimates are NOT generated by AI. They are calculated using deterministic algorithms based on:

  • Curated breed attribute data from authoritative sources
  • Manually researched regional cost data for 8 countries
  • A scoring algorithm based on your questionnaire responses

Limitations of AI Content

AI-generated content has inherent limitations you should be aware of:

  • Not always accurate: AI can produce information that sounds confident but may be incorrect, outdated, or generalized
  • Not professional advice: AI explanations are NOT a substitute for advice from veterinarians, certified trainers, ethical breeders, or animal behaviorists
  • Generalized: AI insights are based on breed averages and may not apply to your specific dog's individual personality or needs
  • Subject to change: AI models and outputs may change over time as the underlying technology evolves

Data Sent to AI Services

When you use AI-powered features, anonymized portions of your questionnaire data and the breed information may be sent to OpenAI to generate the personalized content. We do NOT send your name, email, or any directly identifying information. OpenAI's data handling is governed by their privacy policy.

Your Responsibility

You acknowledge that:

  • AI-generated content is for informational purposes only
  • You will not rely solely on AI insights when making decisions about acquiring or caring for a dog
  • You will consult with qualified professionals (veterinarians, breeders, trainers) for specific advice
  • WhichPuppy and its creators are not liable for decisions made based on AI-generated content

Reporting Issues

If you encounter AI-generated content that is factually incorrect, harmful, or inappropriate, please report it to us at support@thpuppyhq.com. We take these reports seriously and use them to improve the app.
