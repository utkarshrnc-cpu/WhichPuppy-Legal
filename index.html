Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

WhichPuppy ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, Google, or Facebook, we receive your email address and display name. We do not receive or store your password.

Location Data: We collect your country, region, and city (coarse location) to provide region-specific breed cost estimates, availability, and climate-based recommendations. We do not track your precise GPS coordinates continuously. Location is captured once during setup and only updated when you manually change it.

Questionnaire Responses: Your answers to the lifestyle questionnaire are stored securely to generate breed recommendations. This includes information about your living environment, family, lifestyle, budget, and breed preferences.

Subscription Data: We store your subscription status (free or premium) to manage access to premium features. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not see or store your payment information.

Analytics: We collect anonymous usage events (such as questionnaire completion, breed views, and feature usage) to improve the app. These events are not linked to your identity for advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide breed recommendations, personalized cost estimates, and app functionality. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or tracking.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud database) with row-level security policies ensuring you can only access your own data. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Supabase: Database and authentication. Supabase Privacy Policy

OpenAI: AI-powered breed explanations (premium feature only). Questionnaire data may be sent to OpenAI to generate personalized insights. OpenAI Privacy Policy

Apple: Sign in with Apple, subscription management. Apple Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

WhichPuppy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all data, email us at support@thepuppyhq.com with the subject line "WhichPuppy Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Support

For questions, feedback, or support requests, contact us at: support@thepuppyhq.com
